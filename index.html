Empowering Specially Abled Children

Our mission is to enable blind, deaf, mute, and autistic children in rural India to access primary education through technology-driven solutions.

Join the Movement

About Us

Shikhsa Setu is a not-for-profit initiative aimed at leveraging digital tools to provide inclusive and accessible primary education to specially abled children from underprivileged backgrounds in India.

Our Initiatives

  • Audio books and braille content for the visually impaired
  • Sign language video lessons for the deaf and mute
  • Speech-to-text and visual learning apps for autistic learners
  • Mobile classrooms and solar-powered tablets in rural areas
